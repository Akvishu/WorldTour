Hotel Palancio Tangara

Palancio Tangará is exceptionally located in the heart of São Paulo, surrounded by the lush greenery of Burle Marx Park, with easy access to the main business, leisure and culture districts of the city. Palácio Tangará brings the refined European hospitality combined with Brazilian art de vivre to the biggest city of Latin America. Guests are welcomed with a re?ned contemporary décor, bathed in natural daylight throughout its spacious rooms and suites, variety of culinary venues signed by Chef Jean-Georges Vongerichten as well as the state of the art recreational and well-being facilities. A peaceful retreat in a vibrant city. Palácio Tangará has received a host of awards and accolades since its opening, a year ago, which are testimony to the dedication and professionalism of our team. Set in the center of Burle Marx Park nature reserve, this plush hotel in a grand neoclassical building is 5 km from concerts at Citibank Hall and 11 km from Congonhas Airport. Refined rooms have flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi and Nespresso machines, plus cozy sitting and dining areas; some offer park views. Upgraded rooms add furnished balconies or terraces, while suites feature airy living areas with sofas. Room service is available 24/7.
