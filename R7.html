Taleon Imperial Hotel

Taleon Imperial Hotel offers beautiful rooms in the business centre of Saint Petersburg with a stunning view. Featuring a historical architecture, the venue was renovated in 2010. Additionally, you'll find a cash machine, a beauty shop and a cloakroom as well as shuttle service, housekeeping and wake up calls. The excellent location in a shopping area gives quick access to Memorial Stele of Cruiser Aurora. Situated around 1 km from Monument Opened Book, the accommodation is also 2.9 km away from Coffee Museum. Saint Petersburg includes a museum, a palace and a cathedral that are located nearby. The property provides 89 guestrooms appointed with a refrigerator, a cupboard and high ceiling for a pleasant stay in Saint Petersburg. Some units offer views of the Moika River. A shower, a bidet and terry dressing gowns can also be found in each room. A buffet breakfast is served every morning in restaurant at Taleon Imperial Hotel. The Taleon restaurant is known for its Caucasian cuisine. Guests can quench their thirst at the snack bar with a favourite drink. There is a bar serving European food within 100 metres away. Taleon Imperial Hotel Saint Petersburg is about a 25-minute walk from Vitebsky train station. Pulkovo airport is just 25 minutes' drive away.
