THE LODHI HOTEL

The Lodhi Hotel is an Iconic hotel in Pragati Vihar area in New Delhi offering 40 Rooms and Suites offering guests a most exclusive and differentiated luxury hotel experience. Rooms and Suites that large (All rooms are 1000+ sq ft) and offer all modern amenities. Rooms and suites have their own private plunge pool and extensive balconies. All rooms and suites have king size beds, upholstered sofas and chairs, desks, with ambient lighting. Lodhi and Lodhi De Luxe rooms have a combined bedroom/ living area. The bathroom facilities include twin vanities, a shower, bathtub and separate toilet. The luxurious, eastern-modern fusion designed Lodhi Hotel in New Delhi has come into its own as a stand-alone boutique hotel providing a panoramic view of the nearby Lodhi Gardens. The 40 beautiful rooms including a few suites, complete with a plunge pool in each room, a terrace for breakfast and an a la carte restaurant, promises a different experience altogether. Unwind at Elan Restaurant & Bar. The all day Pool Cafe is restricted to hotel guests & club members.
